linguistics jokes that will ruin your day

Noticing a distinct lack of tasteless linguistics jokes on the internet that are not r/BadLinguistics material, I took it upon myself to collect here a bunch of groaners that will torture your soul and get my website in your /etc/hosts blocklists. Hate me all you want, my appreciation of bad jokes and terrible puns will not be affected.

Don't take these beautiful abominations personally; they are meant to be funny, not factual.

a few originals

A linguist walked in to a bar. "The usual," they murmurred. The bartender apologised: "I am new here, sir..." The linguist raised their head, and noticed that they didn't know the bartender indeed. "Sorry," they asked, "is this not The Inn Bar?" The bartender smiled: "No, sir, you must have climbed the stairs in a rush, this is The Inn Pub."

What does a lumberjack and a syntactician have in common?—Both abuse trees for a living.

What do you call a pack of linguists that don't like generative grammar? —A linguist-i-con.

Why didn't the psycholinguist cross the road?—The car stopped by the cops yelled.

OK so if you are not a linguist, you probably don't understand what these jokes are about. If you are a linguist, you're mad at me because none of the jokes characterise observed facts accurately. That's linguistics in a nutshell...

What is the cause of widespread orgasmic dysfunction among linguists? Poverty of stimulus.

If two allophones are very flattering to each other, they are in complimentary distribution.
