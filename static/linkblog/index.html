Zotero plugin that displays associated PubPeer comments

PubPeer is a moderated post-publication review platform where academic journal publications can be discussed outside the peer review process.

Peer review is highly regarded, but like many other highly regarded stuff in academia, is deeply problematic in multiple ways. Publications in and of themselves are highly problematic, and PubPeer thus can only be effective after something manages to go thru that biased and flawed filter, but still, I like a grassroots effort like this.

This Zotero extension displays any existing PubPeer comments for stuff that’s in your Zotero database.
